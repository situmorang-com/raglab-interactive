Password Reset Procedure

To reset your account password, visit the self-service portal at
login.example.com/reset and enter your work email address. You will
receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes.

If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder
or contact the IT helpdesk at helpdesk@example.com.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include one uppercase
letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords cannot be reused
from your last 5 passwords.

If you are locked out after 5 failed login attempts, your account will be
automatically unlocked after 15 minutes, or you may contact IT for an
immediate unlock.

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) cannot be disabled. If you lose access
to your MFA device, contact IT with your employee ID to set up a
temporary bypass code.
