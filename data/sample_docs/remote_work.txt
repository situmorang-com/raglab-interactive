Remote Work Guidelines

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager
approval. Fully remote arrangements require approval from both the
manager and HR, and are evaluated on a role-by-role basis.

Remote employees must be reachable during core hours, 10am-3pm in their
local time zone, and are expected to attend all scheduled meetings with
video on unless otherwise agreed with their manager.

The company provides a one-time $500 stipend for home office equipment
(desk, chair, monitor) for employees working remotely more than 2 days
per week. Submit receipts to finance@example.com within 60 days of
purchase for reimbursement.

All remote work must be performed from a location within the country of
employment unless approved in advance by HR for international remote
work, which is subject to additional tax and legal review.
