Expense Reimbursement Policy

Business-related expenses such as travel, client meals, and approved
software subscriptions are reimbursable. Submit expense reports through
the finance portal within 30 days of the expense being incurred.

Each expense report must include an itemized receipt. Expenses without a
receipt under $25 may be self-certified; anything above $25 requires a
receipt to be reimbursed.

Air travel must be booked in economy class for flights under 6 hours.
Flights over 6 hours may be booked in premium economy with manager
approval. Hotel bookings should not exceed $250/night without prior
approval from finance.

Reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of approval and are
paid out via direct deposit along with the employee's next regular
paycheck.
