New Hire Onboarding Checklist

Before day one: IT provisions a laptop and creates accounts for email,
Slack, and the HR portal. The new hire's manager assigns an onboarding
buddy from the team.

Day one: HR conducts a welcome session covering benefits enrollment,
company policies, and the employee handbook. New hires complete I-9
verification and tax withholding forms.

Week one: New hires meet 1:1 with their manager to review role
expectations and the 30/60/90 day plan. IT security training must be
completed within the first 5 business days.

Benefits enrollment, including health insurance and 401(k) elections,
must be completed within 30 days of the start date. Missing this window
means waiting until the next open enrollment period, except for
qualifying life events.
