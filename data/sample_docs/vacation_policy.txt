Vacation Policy

All full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid vacation per year, credited
at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Part-time employees accrue vacation on a
pro-rated basis according to their scheduled hours.

Vacation requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least two
weeks in advance for requests longer than three consecutive days. Requests
of one or two days may be submitted with 48 hours notice.

Unused vacation days roll over into the next calendar year up to a maximum
of 5 days. Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on January 1st unless a
manager approves a documented exception.

Employees who leave the company are paid out for any unused, accrued
vacation days in their final paycheck, in accordance with state law.
